Addition

Addition is a way to combine numbers to find out how many things there are in total! You take any number and put it with another number, and count them together, and then the new number is the sum! Isn't math fun!

Subtraction

Subtraction is a way of taking a number away from another number and helping us find out how much is left over afterwards! Isn't math fun!

Multiplication

Multiplication is for counting things but in groups! Let's say you have two groups of four ducks! You'd have eight ducks! Isn't math fun!

Division

Division is a way we can share equally with all of our friends! It allows us to split a number into groups.

PEMDAS can be fun!

I'm sure it can be confusing knowing which way you need to do math, so there is a nifty rule! PEMDAS! Everything inside the parentheses, then exponents, then multiplication and division, then addition and subtraction! And you want to handle everything going left to right!
